Stickman Warriors

About Game

Stickman Warriors is a fighting game in which you must fight against other virtual stickman using your very own deadly stickman warrior and your awesome brain. Before you enter the arena you can choose location, customise the appearance of your warrior and give them different guns.

Once you have created your stickman warrior you can start fighting! Select attack type and solve mini tasks to defeat enemies who are trying to kill you. This game is a huge amount of fun and you will find yourself re-entering the arena time and time again to beat your personal records and training your brain!
